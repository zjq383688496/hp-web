关于 创意灯

Creative Mall创意灯是全球最大的文化艺术创意电商。全球的艺术家、设计师、插画师、手工艺者、摄影师、影视动漫公司等文化艺术创意者在创意灯上开设他们的专卖店。产品包括礼品、服装、家居、艺术品和私人定制服务。创意灯作为拥有全球文化艺术创意者专卖店的电商平台已拥有2000万个精致商品和服务可供选择。

公司创立于2009年，目前已成功覆盖全球20个国家，计划至2016年底，将横跨全球60多个国家。我们的核心管理团队来自欧洲、澳洲和中国，从事金融、媒体、图像科技、电子商务等多重领域的跨国运营团队。现已投入3500万在中国自建全球最大产品柔性供应链（全部订单可一件起订），所有产品达到欧美最高标准。2015年底可达1天10万单产量。订单通过DHL在3天内即可送达世界各地。

创意灯Creative Mall作为文化艺术产业垂直电商提供最完整的文创“互联网+“解决方案。全球首家线上线下知识产权保护技术彻底解决盗版问题、产品供应链、电商平台、国际艺术品物流及提供强大的多语言客服团队。我们希望可以通过努力让世界上更多的人能够接触艺术，文化艺术创意者可以得到更多的关注，促进全球文化交流，让这个世界变得更美。

当今物质需求已经到了过剩的年代，创意灯专注解决用户的精神需求。我们吸引了所有对生活品质有要求、文艺范儿、有爱心的用户。我们也希望成为最有爱心、最具社会责任感的电商平台。在不远的未来，公司会将更多企业尽力投入于可持续发展的公益及文化项目上，让Creative Mall的星星艺术燎原于每个角落，点亮全球热爱文化艺术、有精神需求的消费者。

About Creative Mall

Creative Mall is a “Global Artist Marketplace” where Artists from all around the world open their exclusive store through our ecommerce platform. Products includes artworks, home decorations, hand crafts, gift products, and clothing. All custom made to order. Creative Mall currently has over 20 million creative products to choose from.

The company was founded in 2009. Now with presents in 20 countries with estimated growth to 60 countries by end of 2016. Our founding members come from Europe, Australia and China with backgrounds in Arts, Media, Finance, and Technology. We have already invested over 35 million CNY building the largest custom-made product supply chain in the world. Producing on average 100 thousand products per day, All custom-made to order and shipped by DHL international couriers to anywhere around the world.

Creative Mall unlike other online retailers is a complete solution provider to the creative art community. Starting with an ecommerce system especially made for Artists including painters, photographers, graphic designers, hand-craftsmen, sculptors, creative product designers. Our Complete solution provides automated intellectual property protection technology safeguarding online and offline copy-rights for paintings, photographs, designs and more. At the same time act as a global promotion tool for artists to connect with fans, sell artworks and services, all made extremely convenient by leveraging our global logistics and multilingual customer service team. We hope through our effects we can inspire more people to appreciate creative arts, promote international cultural exchange, greater respect for Artists and over all a more beautiful world!

In today’s market people does not need more products! The world needs quality products satisfying spiritual needs able to express individuality. We attract customers who appreciates beauty loves art, protects the environment and believes in contributing back to the community. Creative Mall aims to be a socially responsible company together with our users we want to contribute more to charity organizations and art community projects.
